1 & 2. Comparatives GRAMMAR

Comparative (Equality/Inferiority)

للمساواة أو عدم المساواة بين شيئين.

➤ Equality (=):

as + adj + as
eg: Sami is as young as Said.

➤ Inferiority (≠):

not + as + adj + as
eg: Egypt is not as far as China.

Comparative Form

لمقارنة شيء بشيء آخر (أكبر من، أخطر من).

➤ Short Adjective:

adj + er + than
eg: Algeria is larger than Morocco.

➤ Long Adjective:

More + long adj + than

3. Superlatives & Irregulars GRAMMAR

Superlative Form

التفضيل: لمقارنة واحد بمجموعة (الأذكى، الأجمل).

➤ Short Adjective:

The + short adj + est
eg: Issam is the smartest student in the class.

➤ Long Adjective:

The most + long adj
eg: Iman is the most beautiful girl.

4. Qualifiers

كلمات تسبق الصفة لتقويتها (جداً، للغاية).
(Extremely, so, very) + base adj = strong adj

6. Forming adjectives (affixes)

إضافة حروف في البداية للعكس، أو النهاية للصفة.

➤ Prefixes (opposites / الأضداد):

im, il, ir, in, un, dis
eg: immoral, illegal, irregular, unkind, dishonest.

➤ Suffixes (اللواحق):

Word + ful / less
eg: helpful (متعاون), helpless (عاجز)

5. Sequencers & Linkers PART 1

Time & Arguments

لترتيب الوقت والأفكار في الفقرة (أولاً، ثم، أخيراً).

➤ Time sequencers:

First, next, then, after that, finally.

➤ Arguments linking:

Firstly, secondly, moreover, in addition.

Cause & Effect

للتعبير عن السبب (بسبب) والنتيجة (لذلك).

➤ Cause (لأن / بسبب):

- Nabila went to Anchiano because she want to visit da Vinci's house.

➤ Effect (لذلك / كنتيجة):

- I was tired, so I went to relax.
- She didn't revise. As a result, she didn't succeed.

7. The Present Simple TENSES

Rules

المضارع البسيط: يستخدم للعادات والحقائق اليومية.
  • Affirmative:
    Subj (he, she, it) + stem + s/es
    eg: She goes to school.
  • Negative (النفي):
    Subj (I, you, we, they) + don't
    Subj (he, she, it) + doesn't
  • Question (السؤال):
    Do / Does + Subject + stem ?

8. The Past Simple TENSES

Rules & Markers

الماضي البسيط: لحدث وقع وانتهى في الماضي.

➤ Time markers (دلالات الوقت):

yesterday / last week / in 1996 / ago

➤ Affirmative:

  • Regular verbs (أفعال منتظمة): stem + ed
  • Irregular verbs (أفعال شاذة): (تحفظ من القائمة)
eg: We traveled to Oran. (Regular)
He wrote a story. (Irregular)

Negative & Question

➤ Negative (النفي):

Subject + didn't + stem
eg: He didn't write a short story.

➤ Question (السؤال):

Did + subject + stem ?
eg: Did he write a short story?
MASTER ENGLISH DZ

9 & 10. Future & Perfect TENSES

9. Future Simple

المستقبل: حدث سيقع غداً أو لاحقاً.

Markers: soon / tomorrow / next

  • Subject + will + stem
    I will go to Paris.
  • Subject + won't + stem
    Ahmed won't play.
  • Will + subject + stem ?

10. Present Perfect

المضارع التام: بدأ بالماضي وله أثر الآن.

Markers: ever/never/just/since/for

  • Subj + have/has + pp
    He has just met his friend.
  • Subj + have/has not + pp
  • Have/Has + Subj + pp ?
    Have you ever been to Europe?

11 & 12. Cont. & Passive GRAMMAR

11. Past Continuous

الماضي المستمر: حدث استمر لفترة في الماضي.

➤ Affirmative:

Subj (I, he, she, it) + was + stem(ing)
Subj (you, we, they) + were + stem(ing)
eg: I was playing football yesterday.

12. The Passive Voice

المبني للمجهول: التركيز على المفعول به بدلاً من الفاعل.
Object + was/were + past participle + by + Subj

13 & 14. Narrative & Conditional

13. Narrative (Past)

لربط حدثين في الماضي (بينما / عندما).
While (past continuous) , (past simple)
- While Kateb Yacine was getting interested in school, he started to drift away.
When (past simple) , (past continuous)
- When Lina phoned, Nadia was doing her homework.

14. Conditional Type 1

جملة الشرط: إذا فعلت كذا، سيحدث كذا.
If + (present simple) → (future simple)
eg: If you work hard, you will succeed.
If + (present simple) → (imperative/أمر)
eg: If you want to be a good citizen, help the poor.

15 & 16. Imperative & Advice

15. The Imperative

فعل الأمر: لإعطاء أوامر أو تعليمات.

➤ Positive (أمر إيجابي):

stem (فعل بدون إضافات)
eg: clean the board.

➤ Negative (نهي):

don't + stem
eg: don't clean.

16. Giving Advice

تقديم النصيحة باستخدام (Should / يجب أن).

➤ Positive:

Subject + should + stem
eg: You should help sick people.

➤ Negative:

Subject + shouldn't + stem
eg: You shouldn't be selfish.

Situations of Integration PART 1

1. Itinerary

Last June, my family and I went on a trip all around Algeria. First, we started our itinerary from BBA. We visited El Moqrani Tower, built by Ottomans in 1556. Then, we took a train to Constantine to discover Sidi M’Cid Bridges. After that, we flew to Algiers to see The Great Mosque and the Martyrs Memorial. Finally, we moved to Tipaza, where we saw Roman Ruins. Our trip was amazing.

2. Travelling

Millions of people travel to see other countries, modern cities, and ruins. They travel to enjoy picturesque places or learn about cultures. It's interesting to discover new things, meet people, try different food, and listen to music. Many tourists love taking photographs of sights, mountains, lakes, and forests. People travel by train, plane, ship, or car.

3. My Personal Profile

Hello, my name is Asma. I am 14 years old. I live in a small village with my family. I am calm, honest, and kind. I am interested in reading stories and the Qur'an. I like travelling and cooking, but I dislike listening to music. My dream is to become a doctor because I like helping patients and poor people.

4. My Dream Career

Hi, my name is Sara. When I grow up, I want to become a teacher. I like teaching so much. I want to teach people writing, reading, and speaking. I want to raise a great generation full of kindness, responsibility, and energy. I hope all my dreams come true one day.

Situations of Integration PART 2

5. My Favourite Teacher

During my school life, I have met many teachers, but the best one was my English teacher. He was severe, smart, and skilful. He taught me how to be self-confident, careful, and serious. I respect my teachers and thank them for all what they did.

6. Childhood Memories

Childhood is exciting. I remember playing hide-and-seek with my friends. I didn’t use to buy new toys; I used to make them. We used to eat in the school canteen because our home was far. I used to like Arabic, but I didn’t use to prefer Maths. These are unforgettable memories.

7. School Memories

School is where we learn and meet people. I remember the first day in primary school was hard because I met new friends. I used to sit at the first table. My favourite subject was Maths because I liked counting. My favourite teacher was kind, generous, and calm.

8. The Best Moment

Life is full of events. One of my best moments was when I got my Primary School Certificate. I was playing when my father called me saying: "You got it, dear". We organized a big party and invited friends and family. I have never forgotten this day.

Situations 3 & Verbs FINISH

9. The Worst Moment

One of the worst memories was when I failed in the first year of middle school. I cried a lot. My parents were sad. I didn’t work hard, and I wasted time playing video games. This experience changed my way of thinking, and I became more mature.

10. Good Citizen

To be a good citizen, we should respect the laws and do our duties. We should participate in charity work and help needy people. We must not insult people; we should look after our neighbours and be honest to build a better community.

11. Pollution

Pollution is a danger facing the environment. People throw litters, cut trees, and pollute water. We shouldn’t throw rubbish; we must use bins. Moreover, we must plant trees. Our environment is in danger, and we should save it.
