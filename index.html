1 Imperative (الأمر)

Structure

V + Object
Don't + V + Object

Examples:

  • Help your neighbors.
  • Respect the law.
  • Don't throw rubbish.

شرح بالعربية

يُستعمل فعل الأمر لإعطاء تعليمات أو نصائح أو أوامر.

نستعمل Don't للنهي.

2 Conditional (الشرط)

Structure 1

If + Present Simple → Future

Ex: If you respect others, people will respect you.

Structure 2

If + Present Simple → Imperative

Ex: If you see litter, pick it up!

شرح بالعربية

جملة الشرط تتكون من شرط + نتيجة.

إذا تحقق الشرط → تحدث النتيجة.

Comparatives & Superlatives

1 Comparative

short adj → adj + er + than
long adj → more + adj + than

tall → taller than

beautiful → more beautiful than

2 Superlative

the + adj + est
the most + adj

tall → the tallest

interesting → the most interesting

3 Irregular Adjectives

Positive Comparative Superlative
good better the best
bad worse the worst

7 Past Tense

Past Simple

Used for actions that happened and finished in the past.

Regular: V + ed
play → played
Irregular:
go → went
see → saw

Ex: I visited my grandparents yesterday.

Past Continuous

Used for actions that were happening at a certain time in the past.

was / were + V-ing

Ex: I was studying at 8 pm yesterday.

Ex: They were playing football.

Past Simple: حدث انتهى.      |      Past Continuous: حدث كان مستمراً في وقت معين.

8 Pronunciation

Final "-s"

The final "-s" can be pronounced in 3 ways:

  • /s/
    after voiceless sounds
    stops, books
  • /z/
    after voiced sounds
    plays, lives
  • /iz/
    after s, x, ch, sh, z
    watches, washes

Silent Letters

Some letters are written but not pronounced.

k
know
w
write
b
bomb
l
calm

Tenses
(Future Simple & Present Perfect)

1 Future Simple

Will + V
Examples:
  • I will help you.
  • I will not (won't) go.
  • Will you come?

2 Present Perfect

Subject + have/has + past participle
Keywords: just / already / yet / never / ever
  • I have finished my homework.
  • She has never visited Paris.
